About Us

Crafting Excellence, Preserving Tradition!

Our Story

TimberArt Nepal is more than just a brand; it's a celebration of Nepal's rich artisanal heritage. Founded in Kathmandu, we specialize in creating handcrafted wooden and metal art pieces that embody the essence of Nepali culture. Each product is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who combine traditional techniques with a passion for creativity.

What We Offer

Our collection ranges from intricate wood carvings to elegant metal sculptures, each telling a unique story. Whether you're looking for home decor, personalized gifts, or souvenirs, TimberArt Nepal promises unparalleled craftsmanship and quality.

Our Global Reach

TimberArt Nepal has earned recognition worldwide, delivering authentic Nepali art to customers across the USA, Canada, UK, and Europe. With a commitment to sustainability and cultural preservation, we aim to connect the world with the timeless beauty of Nepali craftsmanship.
